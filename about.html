About Afrilink Corporation

Committed to broad-based economic empowerment and sustainable business growth across South Africa and the African continent.

Company Overview

Afrilink Corporation is a dynamic investment holding company committed to broad-based economic empowerment and sustainable business growth across South Africa and the African continent. Founded under the visionary leadership of Dr Molefe M.D. Mokgatle, the corporation embraces South Africa's diversity and harnesses the strength and abilities of its people, irrespective of colour, creed, or culture.

As a profit-driven enterprise with strict governance principles, Afrilink Corporation focuses on creating measurable value through strategic investments and partnerships that drive both economic empowerment and commercial success. The corporation operates with a clear understanding that sustainable business growth must be accompanied by meaningful economic transformation and community development.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower South African businesses with integrated, compliant, and user-friendly investment and business management solutions that drive meaningful economic transformation while delivering sustainable returns to shareholders and stakeholders.

Our Vision

To become a leading investment corporation in Africa, recognized for our commitment to broad-based empowerment, strong governance practices, and sustainable value creation across diverse economic sectors.

Corporate Values

Our business philosophy is built on four foundational pillars that guide every decision and interaction across our organization.

TRUST

Building lasting relationships through reliability, transparency, and consistent delivery on our commitments. Trust forms the cornerstone of our business relationships, enabling us to forge meaningful partnerships with investors, stakeholders, and communities.

RESPECT

Honoring the dignity, diversity, and contributions of all stakeholders in our business ecosystem. Respect underpins our approach to broad-based empowerment and inclusive business practices.

INTEGRITY

Maintaining the highest ethical standards in all our business practices and decision-making processes. Integrity defines our character as an organization and serves as our compass in navigating complex business environments.

WEALTH CREATION

Generating sustainable value for shareholders, partners, and communities through strategic investments and operational excellence. Wealth creation extends beyond financial returns to encompass broader socioeconomic impact.

Leadership Team

Our experienced leadership team brings decades of expertise across multiple industries and markets, driving strategic vision and operational excellence.

Dr Molefe M.D. Mokgatle

Chief Executive Officer & Chairman

Qualifications

  • Doctor of Business Administration (Paris School of Business, France)
  • Hon. D. Prof Management (Chartered Institute of Management Specialists, USA)
  • MBA Marketing/Strategy (Cardiff Business School, Wales)
  • M.A. Developmental Economics (Schiller International University, London)
  • B.A. Economics (St Peter's College, USA)

Experience

Dr Mokgatle brings 39 years of distinguished executive leadership across multiple industries including Nedbank Ltd, SABMiller, Sowetan Newspaper, SABC TV where he served as Chief Executive of Television Division, AfriSam as Chief Commercial Executive, and Aveng Grinaker-LTA as Executive Director.

He successfully transformed SABC Television from significant losses to substantial profitability while launching commercially viable public broadcasting brands. His international recognition includes addressing the United Nations Media Forum in New York (1998 and 1999), nomination as International Man of the Year by Cambridge Bibliographical Centre (1993 and 1994), and selection as Newsmaker among South Africa's 100 Leaders by The Star newspaper (1996).

Professional Fellowships

  • Chartered Institute of Marketers (UK)
  • Chartered Institute of Directors (UK)
  • Chartered Management Institute (UK)
  • Chartered Doctoral Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Management Specialists (USA)

Mr Sipho Mampe

Director, Financial Management

Qualifications

  • B Com Finance (University of Johannesburg)
  • CIMA Candidate Member
  • EISA Board Exam: Occupational Certificate Tax Technician
  • Registered Tax Practitioner

Experience

Mr Mampe has 16 years of senior financial management experience across banking, insurance, consulting, and project management sectors. His career includes progressive roles at Nedbank from Services Manager to Restructure Controller, senior positions at Corner Life as Manager of Finance and Operations managing three companies within the group, and leadership roles at consulting firms.

He demonstrates exceptional expertise in financial systems management, regulatory compliance, and business process optimization with advanced proficiency in multiple accounting software platforms and financial modeling capabilities. As a Registered Tax Practitioner and Business Accountant in Practice with CIBA, his comprehensive expertise ensures robust fiscal governance across Afrilink Corporation's diverse investment portfolio.
