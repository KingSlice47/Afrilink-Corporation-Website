Investment Holdings

Vacuform 2000 (Pty) Ltd

Automotive Manufacturing

Demonstrates our strategic investment in the automotive manufacturing sector through a specialized component manufacturer that serves South Africa's leading automotive industry. Vacuform 2000 operates from a purpose-built 6,500 m² facility in Rosslyn, Pretoria, manufacturing vacuum-formed, blow moulded, and injection moulded components for major automotive brands including Ford, BMW, Nissan, Toyota, Mercedes Benz, Volkswagen, FORVIA, and Dräexlmaier.

Originally established in 1974 and restructured as Vacuform 2000 (Pty) Ltd in 2000, the company represents a proven track record in precision manufacturing and automotive industry expertise. The company's integration with the Motherson Group since 2011 provides access to global automotive industry networks and advanced manufacturing processes, enhancing competitive positioning in both domestic and export markets.

Website: www.vacuform.co.za

Strider Digital (Pty) Ltd

Digital Talent Outsourcing

Represents our strategic investment in the digital talent outsourcing sector, positioning Afrilink Corporation within the rapidly growing human capital and digital transformation services market. Strider Digital operates as a B-BBEE Level 3 certified digital talent provider, specializing in connecting businesses with top-tier digital professionals including UX designers, software quality engineers, product owners, and technology specialists.

The company serves major South African corporations including leading banks, insurers, and global technology brands, demonstrating exceptional client satisfaction through rigorous Net Promoter Score tracking with scores consistently above 9.6 out of 10. This investment aligns with our commitment to empowerment and skills development, emphasizing employee wellbeing, professional development, and creating opportunities for digital talent to excel.

Website: www.striderdigital.co.za

Afrilink Building and Civils (Pty) Ltd

Construction & Civil Engineering

Represents our investment in the construction and civil engineering sector, positioning us within an industry that is critical to infrastructure development and economic growth. This investment aligns with South Africa's ongoing infrastructure development needs and provides opportunities for skills development, job creation, and economic participation in the construction value chain.

Through this investment, we contribute to the development of essential infrastructure while creating pathways for empowerment and capacity building within the construction industry.

Afrilink Telecoms (Pty) Ltd

Telecommunications

Positions our corporation within the rapidly evolving telecommunications sector, recognizing the strategic importance of connectivity and digital infrastructure in driving economic transformation. This investment reflects our understanding of telecommunications as a key enabler of business growth, social development, and economic inclusion.

The telecommunications sector offers significant opportunities for innovation, service delivery improvement, and the expansion of digital access to underserved communities, aligning with our broader empowerment objectives.

BML Telecoms (Pty) Ltd

Fiber Installation Services

Demonstrates our strategic investment in specialized telecommunications infrastructure through fiber installation services. This investment positions Afrilink Corporation within the critical fiber optic network development sector, which forms the backbone of modern digital communications infrastructure.

BML Telecoms plays an essential role in expanding South Africa's telecommunications capacity, supporting the growing demand for high-speed internet connectivity across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. The fiber installation market represents significant growth potential as businesses and communities increasingly require robust digital infrastructure.

Kp Mining Commodities (Pty) Ltd

Mining & Mineral Resources

Represents our strategic expansion into the mining sector through investments in Botswana's mineral resources industry. Registered in Botswana, the company holds prospecting rights for gold, diamonds, and manganese, positioning Afrilink Corporation within one of Africa's most significant economic sectors.

This investment reflects our commitment to diversifying across the African continent while targeting commodities that are fundamental to both local and global economic development. The mining sector offers substantial opportunities for economic empowerment, skills development, and community participation in resource extraction activities.

Hindsight Consulting (Pty) Ltd

Business Software Development

Represents our strategic investment in the business software development sector, specifically focused on developing accounting and HR systems for Small, Medium and Micro Enterprises (SMMEs). This investment positions Afrilink Corporation within the rapidly growing fintech and business software sector.

The company's focus on SMME-targeted solutions aligns with our commitment to creating accessible business tools that support entrepreneurship and business growth in the South African market, where demand for efficient digital tools continues to expand.

Hindsight Online (Pty) Ltd

Digital Professional Services

Demonstrates our investment in the digital professional services sector, operating as a technology-driven accounting and business administration firm founded in 2017. Based in Randburg, the company specializes in delivering tailored financial and compliance solutions to small and medium-sized enterprises.

Hindsight Online provides cloud-based accounting services, HR management, and business administration solutions designed specifically for South African SMEs. With over seven years of experience serving more than 500 clients across six industries, the company maintains professional affiliations with SAIPA, CIBA, and SAIT while holding QuickBooks ProAdvisor certification.

Investment Focus Areas

Afrilink Corporation specializes in strategic investments across key sectors that are fundamental to Africa's growth and development.

Resources & Infrastructure

The corporation focuses on water and sanitation solutions, essential infrastructure development, and resource management optimization. These investments align with South Africa's critical infrastructure needs while creating opportunities for skills development and economic participation in essential services sectors.

Healthcare

Our healthcare investments encompass service delivery improvement, medical infrastructure development, and health technology solutions. This sector offers significant opportunities to address healthcare challenges while building sustainable business models that serve underserved communities.

Financial Services

The corporation pursues financial inclusion initiatives, investment and wealth management opportunities, and economic empowerment financing solutions. These investments support broader financial sector transformation while creating access to capital for previously disadvantaged entrepreneurs and businesses.

Strategic Ventures

Our strategic venture investments focus on technology and digital solutions, emerging market opportunities, and innovation-driven enterprises. This sector represents the future of business growth and offers significant potential for both returns and empowerment through technology access and skills development.

Our Investment Approach

Our investment strategy targets industries that offer both commercial opportunities and meaningful empowerment potential. We focus on sectors that are fundamental to economic development and provide pathways for skills development, job creation, and community participation.

Each investment is evaluated not only for its financial returns but also for its contribution to broader economic transformation and sustainable development across the African continent.
